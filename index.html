Overview

PetGuardian is a comprehensive pet monitoring and tracking application that combines GPS location tracking, real-time monitoring, and interactive communication features to keep your pet safe and connected.

Main Interface Features

1. Real-time Location

  • Interactive Map: Displays your pet's current location on a live map
  • GPS Tracking: Provides accurate location updates when your pet is outdoors
  • Indoor Positioning: Uses Wi-Fi and other signals for indoor location tracking
  • Location History: View where your pet has been throughout the day
How to Use:
  • The map updates automatically when the app is open
  • Tap and zoom on the map to get a closer view of your pet's location
  • Pull down to refresh for the latest location update

2. Collar Status

  • Battery Level: Shows current battery percentage
  • Battery Indicator: Green indicates good battery level
  • Connection Status: Displays if the collar is connected to the network
  • Signal Strength: Shows GPS and cellular signal quality
Battery Management:
  • Charge the collar when battery drops below 20%
  • Battery typically lasts 5-7 days depending on usage
  • Enable power-saving mode to extend battery life up to 30 days

3. Lost Mode

Emergency Tracking Mode
  • Activates enhanced tracking when your pet goes missing
  • Provides more frequent location updates during emergency
  • Uses all available networks for maximum tracking coverage
  • Sends immediate alerts when pet leaves safe zones
How to Activate:
  1. Tap the Lost Mode toggle switch
  2. Confirm activation in the popup dialog
  3. The collar will enter emergency tracking mode
  4. Deactivate when your pet is found safe

Setting Up Safe Zones

Geo-fencing

  1. Tap the map area to access location settings
  2. Create Safe Zones: Draw boundaries around safe areas (home, yard, dog park)
  3. Set Zone Names: Label each safe zone for easy identification
  4. Configure Alerts: Choose notification types for zone entries/exits
  5. Save Settings: Confirm and save your geo-fence settings

Zone Types:

  • Home Zone: Primary safe area around your residence
  • Play Areas: Dog parks, beaches, or recreational areas
  • Restricted Zones: Areas where your pet should not go

Collar Setup and Maintenance

Initial Setup

  1. Charge the Collar: Fully charge before first use
  2. Download App: Install PetGuardian app on your smartphone
  3. Create Account: Register with email and password
  4. Pair Device: Follow in-app instructions to connect collar
  5. Fit Collar: Adjust for comfortable, secure fit on your pet

Proper Collar Fitting

  • Snug but Comfortable: Should fit snugly without being too tight
  • Two-Finger Rule: You should be able to fit two fingers between collar and neck
  • Contact Points: Ensure GPS and communication components touch your pet's neck
  • Regular Checks: Check fit regularly, especially for growing pets

Troubleshooting

Connection Issues

  • Poor GPS Signal: Move to open area, away from buildings
  • App Not Connecting: Check Wi-Fi/cellular connection, restart app
  • Collar Offline: Ensure collar is charged and in coverage area
  • Delayed Updates: Location updates may be delayed in poor signal areas

Audio/Video Issues

  • No Audio: Check microphone permissions and volume settings
  • Poor Video Quality: Ensure good cellular/Wi-Fi signal strength
  • Delayed Feed: Video may have slight delay due to transmission

Safety Guidelines

Important Safety Information

  • Remove for Rest: Don't leave collar on for more than 12 hours daily
  • Regular Breaks: Give your pet collar-free time indoors
  • Skin Irritation: Check regularly for any signs of irritation
  • Age Requirements: Not recommended for pets under 6 months old
  • Weight Requirements: Ensure collar is appropriate for your pet's size

Emergency Procedures

  1. Pet Goes Missing: Immediately activate Lost Mode
  2. Contact Authorities: Notify local animal control and shelters
  3. Use All Features: Utilize Listen, Talk, and Camera functions to locate pet
  4. Check Activity Log: Review recent activity for clues about disappearance
  5. Share Information: Use location data to coordinate search efforts

Support & Best Practices

Daily Use Best Practices

  • Morning Check: Verify collar is charged and connected
  • Set Activity Goals: Encourage healthy exercise levels
  • Monitor Regularly: Check location and status throughout day
  • Evening Review: Review daily activity and any alerts

Customer Support

  • In-App Help: Tap help icon for tutorials and FAQs
  • Online Support: Visit manufacturer's website for updates
  • Contact Support: Use in-app contact form for technical issues
  • User Community: Join online forums for tips and advice
